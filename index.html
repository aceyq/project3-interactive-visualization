Design Rationale & Process

  • Linked views: A global time-series with brushing supports temporal focus; the brush drives interpretation alongside a late‑century summary.
  • Scenario filters: Pill toggles enable quick comparisons across SSPs without clutter.
  • Encodings: Position over time for trend (lines), magnitude comparison for regions (bars), density for coverage (heatmap).
  • Details-on-demand: Tooltips surface exact values; legends and labels call out baselines.
