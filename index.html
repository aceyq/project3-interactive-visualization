How do projected changes in global temperature and precipitation under different CMIP6 scenarios help us understand future climate patterns and their potential impact on global vegetation?

Global Trend Over Time • Brush to select years

Interactive line chart showing global mean change over time for selected scenarios. Use the brush to focus on a window; hover over points for exact values.

Design Rationale & Process

  • Linked views: A global time-series with brushing supports temporal focus; the brush can inform interpretation of late-century summaries.
  • Scenario filters: Pill toggles enable quick comparisons across SSPs without visual clutter.
  • Encodings: Position over time for trends (lines) and magnitude comparison for regions (future bar chart), supporting comparison across scenarios.
  • Details-on-demand: Tooltips surface exact values for temperature and precipitation by year and scenario.

Built with D3.js for DSC 106 Project 3. Data: CMIP6 (preprocessed CSVs placed in /data).
